Overview

This Webapp explores predicting food security using *Multiple Linear Regression*.

Learn more »

Sub-Saharan African

The region we have chosen the Sub-Saharan African region as that region has the world's highest rate of malnutrition. It also has one of the world's highest poverty rates. This shows that this area faces high risk of food insecurity.

NGO

This Webapp is meant to be used by a Humanitarian Food Aid NGO. Since the model is trained with data from 10 Sub-Saharan countries, it would work best for that region. Using metrics that would reflect the factors affecting food insecurity, we hope to predict where to send food to as a Humanitarian Food Aid NGO.

Food Security. Affordability. Availability.

Using similar metrics to the Econonmist, we chose to predict food affordability and availability as we feel that would be highly reflective of the food security situation in those countries. Ultimately, we wish to alleviate the food security issues in those countries, even if it is in the short run.
